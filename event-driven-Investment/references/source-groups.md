## Usage Notes

1. Always prioritize primary sources (official data, regulatory filings) over media.
2. Cross-check key factual claims with at least two independent sources.
3. Note publication date for all sources — timeliness matters for event-driven analysis.
4. Mark claims sourced only from social media or unverified KOLs as `待验证`.
