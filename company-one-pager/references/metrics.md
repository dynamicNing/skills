# Financial Metrics Reference

Quick definitions for metrics used in the one-pager template.

## Profitability

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | Pricing power and production efficiency |
| Net Margin | Net Income / Revenue | Bottom-line profitability after all costs |
| ROE | Net Income / Shareholder Equity | Return on shareholder capital |
| ROIC | NOPAT / Invested Capital | Return on all capital (debt + equity) |

## Growth

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue CAGR | (Rev_n / Rev_0)^(1/n) - 1 | Compound annual growth rate |
| EPS Growth | (EPS_n - EPS_n-1) / EPS_n-1 | Per-share earnings momentum |

## Leverage & Liquidity

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|
| Debt/Equity | Total Debt / Total Equity | Financial leverage |
| Current Ratio | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | Short-term liquidity |
| Interest Coverage | EBIT / Interest Expense | Ability to service debt |

## Cash Flow

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|
| FCF | Operating CF - CapEx | Cash available after maintenance |
| FCF Yield | FCF / Market Cap | Cash generation vs. valuation |
| OCF/Net Income | Operating CF / Net Income | Earnings quality (>1 is good) |

## Valuation

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|
| P/E | Price / EPS | Price per unit of earnings |
| P/S | Market Cap / Revenue | Price per unit of sales |
| P/B | Price / Book Value per Share | Price vs. net asset value |
| EV/EBITDA | Enterprise Value / EBITDA | Valuation independent of capital structure |
| PEG | P/E / EPS Growth Rate | Growth-adjusted valuation |
